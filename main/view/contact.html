Movie Search App:


This app lets you search through the OMDB Api to find other movies, series, and episodes that released with the same year and name.



Laid out in symmetrical columns, it shows you everything in your search specifications.

Car Inventory App:


Makes a nice and easy to use inventory app. You could easily change some words to make it anything that you would need.

Employee App:


This app lets you create a list of employees along with their title and salary.



When you add all the people you want to add you can click on the "Print all to console" button and see everyone you added.

Shopping list:


This lets you add and delete items to a list.
